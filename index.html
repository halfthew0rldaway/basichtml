Welcome!

How’s it goin’

Ah, another website—because the internet clearly didn’t have enough of those. But hey, you’re here now, so might as well make yourself comfortable. I’m bLeu (yeah, it’s a nickname), a digital hoarder, technician, and vector artist who spends way too much time tinkering with software, breaking things just to fix them again, and pretending to understand AI like everyone else these days.

Technology is both my passion and my problem. I’ll spend hours fixing a device that wasn’t really broken, refuse to delete a single file because “I might need it in 10 years,” and hoard unfinished projects like they’re rare collectibles. Somewhere between reviving gadgets and designing vectors, I convince myself this chaos is just "organized innovation." My storage is always full, my projects never-ending, and my patience constantly running on low battery—but hey, that’s just the price of being a functional digital mess.
